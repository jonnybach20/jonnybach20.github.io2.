Geographical Information Systems

Geographical Information Systems were a key component of Master's program. My modules taught me proficiency in QGIS, I learned how to digistise and manipulate spatial data, subjecting it to various types of data analysis. I receievd a distinction in this module without any prior expereince in GIS, demonstrating my ability to easily acquire new skills.

For my first assignment, I had to submit my solutions to solve various complex geospatial problems using a variety of techniques.

I was then tasked with creating a research poster as a way of scientifically communicating the effect climate change can have on the distribution of an invasive species, by using results from species distribution models.

I have continued to develop my GIS skills in my role as a technical support officer. I support my team by preparing graphical representation of data in relation to developments in the National Park in the form of maps

I have also been improving my efficiency in ArcGIS products by engaging in self-directed learning. This year I have completed two ESRI MOOC's titled 'Spatial Data Science: The New Frontier in Analytics' and 'Imagery in Action'.
