Statistical Analysis

The use of research methods is very important within social sceinces. Throughout my undergraduate degree, I developed my understanding of research methods and data analysis, and was taught the appropriate steps in designing a research project. As aforementioned, this training facilitated my success in my final year dissertation, due to my design of a sound research project and my usage of appropriate statistical analysis.

In my Master's we looked at how geographical thinking can be injected into social and environmental data analysis, informing policy implications. Thus, we manipulated a large data-set to lead our own inquiry into a social phenomenon within a geographical region. I utilised the responses of over 3,500 participants and used multiple methods of data anlysis within SPSS, such as logistic regression, to interpret the findings and produce a report outlining the possible policy implications around them.
