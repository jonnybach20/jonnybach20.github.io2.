About me


I am a 23-year-old young professional who currently works as a Technical Support Officer within the Strategic Policy team for the Pembrokeshire Coast National Park Authority. I am from Pembrokeshire originally, but I studied in Bristol for four years. Now that I have some expereince at an entry-level, I am hoping to relocate back to Bristol.
I am excited by the Planning Research and Monitoring Officer role, as I am seeking a role where I can develop and utilise my existing skills. I am a capable individual who embraces change and enjoys new challenges, and I am really interested in this role, as demonstrated by my admittedly amateur attempt at coding and website design!
Navigating this webpage you will find links to some of my professional and academic experiences that I believe show my suitability for this role with the Bristol City Council's Strategic City Planning Team. Feel free to search for these from the home page!
